Guard Call

Call companion demo · friendly family safety

Demo idea

A quiet safety layer that works while the call is still happening.

This prototype simulates a real phone call. Guard Call does not replace the call interface — it listens in the background and surfaces a calm companion card the moment something starts to look like fraud.

📞

Live transcript

Speech is captioned in real time. Risky phrases are highlighted as they appear.

🔔

Scaling alert

Icon, color and percentage grow with the risk so the user notices without being scared.

🛡️

Family support

Above 85% risk the phone vibrates and one tap notifies a trusted contact.

Pitch line

Guard Call stays quiet during normal calls and only becomes visible when the user needs help. It explains the risk, suggests what to say, and helps prevent unsafe decisions — without ever taking over the conversation.
